# HubSpot ROL Calculator
**Business Intent:** Visualizes the "Revenue Leakage" caused by slow lead response times to drive urgency for automation.
**Primary Pain Point:** "Speed to Lead" anxiety and lost deals due to manual follow-up.
**Key Metric:** Annual Revenue Leaking (ARL).
**Inputs:** Monthly Leads, Average Deal Value, Current Close Rate.
**Outputs:** Projected Revenue Recovery with AI Automation.
## Overview
A client-side calculator that quantifies the cost of inaction. It demonstrates how increasing speed-to-lead via automation impacts the bottom line.
